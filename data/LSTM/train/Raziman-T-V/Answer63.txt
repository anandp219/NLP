His championing of the Dalit Buddhist movement was one reason caste Hindus had to worry and start caring - at least namesake - about Dalits. He once famously said to Gandhi  "Mahatmaji  I have no country". If Dalits feel any less like that today  it is thanks to Ambedkar.Beyond Dalits  his effect on the laws of India is also huge. Being the first law minister and the chairman of the Constitution drafting committee  he had a major role in ensuring that the constitution was the perfect social document for India.
