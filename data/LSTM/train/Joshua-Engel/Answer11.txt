  Security is practically always antithetical to usability. Making it harder for thieves to get in nearly always makes it harder for authorized users to get in. In fact  much of the time  it makes things much harder for real users while making it only trivially more difficult for thieves. The nature of health care makes it necessary to share things. A lot of people are involved: the patient  their primary doctor  specialists  nurses  pharmacists  and an army of others who really just want to get the information they need so that they can treat the patient.
