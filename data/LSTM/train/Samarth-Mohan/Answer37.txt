  The Lives of Others (2007) [1]  This is a powerful and quiet film  that follows the story of a member of East Germany's infamous Stasi; while he himself  in the course of his secret service duties  voyeurs into the lives of a playwright and his mistress. As he watches their lives unfold  every single little secret laid bare in front of him  he starts to internalize their happiness  and pain and suffering  providing a delightful juxtaposition with his own empty life. This movie is dark and intense  and relentlessly fascinating.Gattaca (1997)  A movie that does everything right  which is as much entertaining as is a great representation of what some call the "human spirit".
