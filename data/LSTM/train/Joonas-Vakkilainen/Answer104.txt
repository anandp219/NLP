That is  people don't address other people if they don't need to. In positive politeness  you have to tell people compliments but in negative politeness  you avoid saying unnecessary things to people who are not in your inner circle. People don't want to intervene in others' businesses. Non-Nordic people may find this rude  but we don't see it in that way. There are cultural places where people behave differently. Sauna has a relaxed atmosphere  which is a good place for foreigners to make contact with Finns if they can't do it elsewhere.
