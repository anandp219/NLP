He is then taken up by a humble charioteer in the service of the king of Hastinapur and his wife  and this prince grows as an extraordinary boy in ordinary surroundings  unaware of his identity... just how many stories do we have which run on similar lines? It is a classic case that appeals to our mindset.Karna grows along with the Kauravas  and commands their respect  and Duryodhan's friendship due to his virtues and inherent qualities. He is that hard working  brilliant  self-made but underprivileged boy whom everyone admires.
