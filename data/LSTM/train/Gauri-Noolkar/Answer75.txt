Across religions and regions  one thing that really binds Indians is their treatment of guests. For a non-Indian  it can get awkward and over-bearing  but for Indians  guests and their comfort are of prime importance. Many non-Indian friends of mine who have stayed with us or other Indian families have appreciated  time and again  the warm hospitality they receive.
