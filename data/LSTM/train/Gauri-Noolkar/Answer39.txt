  It is indeed a noble thought to teach and inspire children to leave behind an extraordinary footprint on the sands of time. The tricky part is making sure the thought is conveyed and received in its purest form.My dream... your dream?As a sensible  mature parent perhaps the most difficult thing to do is to ensure that you don't entangle your dreams with theirs. This is different from inculcating your values in them  or sharing your ideas and goals of life with them. There is a very thin line between inspiring and pressurizing your children to achieve the extraordinary.
