  Vito knew that the power of his illicit empire came from personal connections  friendships and relationships with all manner of people  from lowly boarders needing help to pay the rent to judges  senators  congressmen  etc. The novel explores this in more detail. Over the decades  many people came to Vito for advice or help. He helped kids get into college and advised them on careers. Some became judges  politicians  high ranking police officials and more. They never forgot the kindness of their "godfather" and were glad to help him in return.
