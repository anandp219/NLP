A strong case could be made to classify it as strictly a poem  a work of performance art  or a musical composition. What may tilt the scales of identification in favor of music is the intent by the composer during the writing process. Returning to The Grande dizionario della lingua italiana definition  there is deliberate and organized combining sounds. These are indeed coordinated in time and space  and produced by the medium of voice. The final part of the definition  structured according to pitch level  duration  intensity  and timbre; science of sound subdivided into melody  harmony  and rhythm  may be seen as an elaboration common in the application of sounds organized in time and space  but should not be understood as a restriction.
