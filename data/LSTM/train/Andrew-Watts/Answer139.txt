The influence of Cage  however  extends far beyond his time with the Sonic Arts Union. Lucier noted during his lecture at the 2015 Ostrava Days Composition Institute that Cage exerted a sort of cult of personality amongst the experimental music scene in the 1960s. Lucier expressed great admiration for Cage's compositional philosophies  particularly that he is not interested in forcing musical relationships  but wishes to just allow the natural phenomena to happen.
