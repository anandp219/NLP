Symphony orchestras and publishing companies upload program notes too. These notes are often written by the composer  a music director  music critic  or another specialist and concisely tell you what you need to know. Also  the BBC has produced a number of high quality documentaries about the lives and works of several famed classical composers. These are certainly worth checking out and will provide a great deal of insight.
