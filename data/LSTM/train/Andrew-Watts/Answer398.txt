(Sarkisyan)Akira Nishimura (1953- )  Nishimura principally employs heterophony  a characteristic device of Asian traditional music  thereby subtly transforming the intervals  rhythm and melody of his dense multi-layered textures. Though similar to the micropolyphony of Ligeti  an Asian perspective informs his technique. (Narazaki)Erkki-Sven Tr (1959- )  When the solo lines [of regilaul] play against the larger instrumental body  it is often in the complex textures of Ligetian micropolyphony. (Anderson)
