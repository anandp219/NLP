I use some chords  which have relationships to the harmonic series. I use them because I love them and because they sound great. But I never used spectral analyses [...] I trust my sensations. I try to find out how sound and colour and time and dynamics fit together. I do not have any system. I do not trust systems at all.Nevertheless  the ideas put forth by spectral composers have been important in Haas' own compositional practice. He has further explained the creative relationship he has with spectral concepts in the Three Questions for Sixty-Five Composers:Creative processes are sometimes set in train by verbal statements by composers which may have rather an intriguing relationship with the compositions to which they refer.
