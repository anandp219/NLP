  [Image via Terry Riley Celebration  St George's Bristol & Bristol Old Vic | New music reviews  news & interviews]In C (1964) is considered to be the first major work of Minimalism in music. As with many  if not most  stylistic movements in Western music  the fine art world arrived there first. In France between 1947 and 1948  Yves Klein conceived his Monotone Symphony (1949  formally The Monotone-Silence Symphony) that consisted of a single 20-minute sustained chord followed by a 20-minute silence  a precedent to both La Monte Young's drone music and John Cage's 433.
