As you progress with sit-ups and the like  you will want to find ways to make them harder. Planks can be made harder by lifting your limbs off the ground (or putting your feet on an elevated surface)  while leg raises can be made harder by hanging from a bar. Sit-ups can be made harder by making them unilateral  holding out your arms and holding on to weight.
