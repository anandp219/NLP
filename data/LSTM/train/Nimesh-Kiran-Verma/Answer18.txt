If we really could plan our future lives for our future selves  this would gut our lives' journeys of the experiences that make us grow and develop as people.What about empathy? The worst part of the Law of Attraction is that is discourages the kinds of practices that can help the most isolated and miserable among us feel some relief from suffering. Among the most trans formative and healing moments in psychotherapy are those in which the therapist can step into the client's pain - can share in it for a time - so that the client feels another's understanding  compassionate presence and feels not so alone.
