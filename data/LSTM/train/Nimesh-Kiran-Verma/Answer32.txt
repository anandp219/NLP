Arjun got peeved with Krishna because he thought he was doing better than Karna. Krishna said "Your chariot is being driven by me  Vishwaroopam and has Lord Hanuman on your flag protecting it. Pushing back your chariot even slightly is an impossible task with us two deities protecting it. In spite of that  Karna is pushing it back with every attack. "Decided the outcome of duel in a way.
