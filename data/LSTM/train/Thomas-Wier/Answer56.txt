'The bear is' and 'the bears are' would thus respectively be a ba and ar da. It is thus the specific combination of exponents -- actual affixes -- that determines which gender any given noun belongs to. The problem with this is that  for the analyst  it seems to be missing a generalization that the last three of these genders  genders VI  VII and VIII  only consist of a small number of nouns -- 25 total for those three combined -- and they might better be listed as lexical exceptions in the grammar.
