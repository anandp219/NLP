  The path and the goal are one.The minute you make room for anything out of the ordinary to occur  the second you get out of the way from your mundane way of life- you open yourself up for anything to happen. Dreaming of that which is possible  which seems impossible- making that dream or goal or whatever it is conscious- is the start of your path.The American actor who played Superman  Christopher Reeve  dreamt of walking again after a terrible horse riding accident left him paralyzed in all four quadrants of his body.
