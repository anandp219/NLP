"Who is this?" I asked one of my friends. And she told me  "That is Elie Wiesel. Have you ever read Night? "I had not. So my friend suggested I pick up the book and read it from start to finish. "It won't take you long " she said  but she was wrong. I savored it and let it penetrate me for days with its horrors. The first sentence that I underlined grabbed me. I had been more or less attracted to such life positive books  stories to do with championing over the human spirit. Mild spirituality. Self-help.
