Did you move for work? Your workplace provides you instantly with a social milieu which you may choose to like or not but it is there to explore. Observe your surroundings. Do you see public houses  or other spaces that welcome everyone? Find out more about them. Note their timings and their location with respect to your schedule and location. Identify where your interests coincide with your context both the given one and the one your surroundings outside school or work provide you. Explore for some time.
