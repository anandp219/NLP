  By keeping it simple.Generally  the part that makes a pizza heavy is the dough. The key to a light and tasty pizza is:Use as little (correctly proven) dough as possible Only a few high quality ingredientsCooked at a high temperature (preferably with a pizza stone)To prove pizza dough to an optimal level  its best to leave it somewhere warm (around 21C to 24C) overnight or until the dough has at least doubled in size. This is important as it creates good conditions for the yeast to break down the starch into sugar for it too consume.
