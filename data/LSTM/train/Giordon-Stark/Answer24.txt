Tier 0 is at CERN itself and consists in large part of thousands of commercially bought servers  both PC-style boxes and  more recently  blade systems looking like black pizza boxes  stacked in row after row of shelves. Computers are still being purchased and added to the system. The data passed to Tier 0 by the LHC data-acquisition systems will be archived on magnetic tape.Tier 0 will distribute the data to the 12 Tier 1 centers  which are located at CERN itself and at 11 other major institutes around the world.
