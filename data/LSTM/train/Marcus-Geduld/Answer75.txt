To be productive  they also need to learn common libraries and frameworks like JQuery  Underscore  Angular  Backbone  Ember  and React. (A professional JavaScript developer should have at least some familiarity with all of them! )They also needs to understand tooling  e.g. build systems  such as Grunt  Gulp  and Webpack.
