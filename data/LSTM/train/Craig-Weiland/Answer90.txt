  Agent Smith did not die. He  along with Neo  transcended together into their next state of being.Neo and Smith are innately connected with each other. The Oracle describes Smith to Neo as such: "He is you. He is your opposite  your negative. The result of the equation trying to balance itself out." Smith's path is deeply woven into Neo's. They are inseparable.Neo knew that both he and Smith had reach their respective limitsNeo in the real world and Smith in the Matrix. Neo had penetrated the Machine City and decimated all defenses that the Machines threw at him... blindfolded.
