Let's say a robber steals all the goods of a person. We've created economic equality without any market forces. Of course we've government organizations to redress grievances in such cases. However such created economic inequality becomes a major problem when it's institutionalized through a mixture of legal  social and other discrimination. Consider for example  a certain race/ gender/ religion/ caste is discriminated against and it's people considered dumb/ untouchable/ violent etc which leads them to be seen unfavorably.
