It is left to every individual the connotation they choose while singing it. National anthem is nothing but a symbol. "The truth" associated with the symbol doesn't matter- partly because it can never be known. These symbols serve purposes. National anthem serves the purpose of instilling pride in citizens while singing it- which Jana Gana Mana does pretty well. It depends on the singer which connotation they want to choose. About the alternatives "Vande Matram" was proposed as the National anthem- but certain religious groups had issues with it citing idol worship(Maa Durga) connotations.
