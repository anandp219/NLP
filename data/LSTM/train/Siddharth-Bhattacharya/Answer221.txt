We can call it a detective sci-fi. But if one wants to go deep  there's endless at offer. That too in only 37 episodes of about 20 minutes. The character of Yagami Light offers varied perspectives on wide-range of ethical conflicts: an intelligent  hard-working student dreaming of changing the world and weeding out evil if given the opportunity actually finds one. The series is a glimpse of the wide array of possibilities of how a well-meaning individual can detract from good causes with the availability of power.
