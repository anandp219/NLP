Very often laptops are supplied  but this isnt essential for hot-desking as long as desktops are set up for multiple user logins from the network. Pros (some perceived some real)Saves money. Hot-desking is popular in companies with a large number of consultants  as consultants frequently work away from the office on client sites or they work from home  so large savings can be made by having less desks than employees. Consultancies like to maintain nice head offices in the centre of their capital cities  and this will be expensive in cities like London.
