  When it comes to learning any language  there is nothing special about English. It needs the same kind of effort as learning other languages. The formula is usually straightforward: immersion and performance.Immersion: This is about how much exposure to that language the learner gets. In case of English  this is delightfully easy. We are surrounded by English content. Movies  radio  TV shows  songs  books  magazines  what not. No matter what your interest  you are sure to find English content in it.
