And sure  where you go to college can matter. All things being equal  going to a top school will help you in life. If only to boost your confidence and get your resume past the screeners. Also  top schools generally have grade inflation  and at these elite Schools It's Almost Impossible To Fail. It can be easier to get a perfect GPA at some of the top schools than it is at the larger State schools. However  it's not so simple. Or easy. Luck  skill  and resiliency will also matter  and ultimately they will play a far greater role in your success than which college you went to.
