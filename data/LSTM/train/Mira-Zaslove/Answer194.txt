You want to see the incoming talent get better not worse. If the company starts hiring C players to replace A players in the important roles  that is a red flag.Also  if people who know more than you do in critical roles  CFO  Chief Counsel  Head of Product / Engineering / Sales  etc.  unexpectedly leave  or are suddenly fired with no explanation  these can be red flags.A personal red flag of mine is when leadership repeatedly talks poorly about current and ex-employees.
