Start from a healthy place. Then continue to eat well  with some well-deserved splurges. Exercise through your pregnancy. Drink lots of water. Eat fruits  veggies  and whole foods. Stay away from too much salt  sugar  and white flour. Something that really surprised me: I came home from the hospital looking worse  than I did going in.
