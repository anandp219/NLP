  Power is dangerous in the Game of Thrones. As Cersei Lannister reminds Ned Stark during their final confrontation:"When you play the game of thrones  you win or you die. There is no middle ground." Jon Snow  by becoming the Lord Commander of the Nights Watch  put himself in a position of power  a precarious position in Game of Thrones. Jeor Mormont  the 997th Lord Commander of the Nights Watch  who led the Great Ranging was also betrayed and killed by his own men in the mutiny at Craster's Keep.
