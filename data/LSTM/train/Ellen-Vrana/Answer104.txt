Triggers memory-rich primary content (MRPC)I made up this acronym to mean my actual memories which I pop into the stories from time to time to give the scenes color and authenticity. My memories are triggered by reading  never writing. Because when reading  I'm expanding; when writing  I'm narrowing.Most important  however  reading while I write provides crucial mental and emotional rejuvenation.Writing is the act of pulling  extracting  building  tearing down  rebuilding. It feels violent and disruptive.
