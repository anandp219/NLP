When he was younger he had an accident which changed his brain. He could no longer tell how people were reacting to him. So he made a lifelong study of body actions and what they mean. For instance  he told me. "If you want people to like you  then very subtly mirror all of their moves. If they cross their legs  cross your legs a few seconds later. If they wipe their forehead  wipe your forehead." "Won't they notice?" I asked. "No " he said  "People are too focused on themselves to consciously notice what you are doing.
