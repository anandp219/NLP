He did 10 000 experiments. Everything we do today is an experiment. Sometimes it's disappointing if an experiment doesn't work out. But that's ok  we experiment again. You can use this meta-habit when you build a business. Don't build a business and see if it works. Try this idea first: put up ads on Facebook or Craigslist or wherever for your product or serve. Use a small budget. See if people click. If a lot of people click then you might have a good business idea. If nobody clicks  then move on to the next idea before you build anything.
