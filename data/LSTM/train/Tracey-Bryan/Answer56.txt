All are entitled to equal protection against any discrimination in violation of this Declaration and against any incitement to such discrimination.Everyone has the right to an effective remedy by the competent national tribunals for acts violating the fundamental rights granted him by the constitution or by law.No one shall be subjected to arbitrary arrest  detention or exile.Everyone is entitled in full equality to a fair and public hearing by an independent and impartial tribunal  in the determination of his rights and obligations and of any criminal charge against him.
