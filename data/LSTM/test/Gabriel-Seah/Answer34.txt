With the Party abandoning its Communist ideology  the unifying national ideology was to be "patriotism"  i.e. stirring up historic grievances against other countries to unify the People under the banner of the Party:The party blamed Tiananmen on dangerous foreign ideas and the mission of public history became to demonstrate that foreigners had been behind the countrys major setbacks since the early 19th century. The people were to be persuaded that the party was all that stood between China and foreigners ill-intentions and that Chinas long history and superior culture made it an exception to the widely observed phenomenon that  once they achieve a certain standard of living  people tend to demand a say in how they are governed.
