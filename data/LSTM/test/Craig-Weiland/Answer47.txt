  Their parents' perspective.Often when a teenager will want something or want to do something and the parent will deny it to them  the teen will get angry. "Why can't I [have it]?" they ask. The parents' response often comes in the form of  "Because I said so. "This is infuriating to the teen. The teen knows only their own personal experience and the short-term need that the thing they want will address. They are incapable of seeing a bigger picture  one that includes not only this short-term thing but the possible long-term ramifications of it or various possible unintended consequences that stem from it.
