  Judo has different katas for every aspect of the art.Nage no kata demonstrates 15 throws: three of each waza  performed both in migi and hidari. Uke starts the techniques by taking a grip or by hitting.Katame no kata shows grappling techniques: holdings  chokes and locks. Uke performs attempts of escape  which tori blocks.Kime no kata is the art of old-fashioned self-defence. It is also called the kata of determination. Uke attacks with bare-hand hits  kicks  grapplings  a knife and a sword. Tori responds with techniques that eventually end with a lock  a stroke or a throw.Kdkan goshin jutsu is the art of modern self-defence.
