Under this  the VLC playback parameters can be passed as <vlc:option> tags.For example  here is how to play a youtube file as audio-only from a given start time to stop time: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 910<track> <location>https://www.youtube.com /watch?v=bd2UrN5IJYc</location> <title>Jessy</title> <extension application="http://www.videolan.org/vlc/playlist/0"> <vlc:option>network-caching=2000</vlc:option> <vlc:option>start-time=6.000</vlc:option> <vlc:option>stop-time=325.000</vlc:option> <vlc:option>no-video</vlc:option> </extension></track>(Just edit the location to get it working correctly  Quora does not let me put youtube links without it expanding to the embedded video plugin.)
