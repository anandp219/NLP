For most people  at a certain point  the goal is just to come to a conclusion and move on. But  for the most effective negotiators  the goal is always to get the best deal possible given all the circumstances. Impatient take it or leave it negotiating rarely achieves the optimal outcome.Finally  most negotiations involve a degree of gamesmanship and top negotiators have honed pattern recognition and are comfortable with negotiation strategies.
