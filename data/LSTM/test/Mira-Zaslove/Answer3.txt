"Give Me Liberty or Give Me Death" served as a popular battle cry for the American Patriots  Continental Army and Minutemen. "A House Divided Against Itself Cannot Stand." Abraham Lincoln at the Illinois Capitol  1858. (Source: Abraham Lincoln Giving A Speech Picture Gallery)Abraham Lincoln (1809 1865) was the 16th President of the United States. Lincoln declared this memorable one-liner when he was accepting the Illinois Republican Party's nomination for US senator. This simple statement clearly articulated Lincolns views on keeping the Union together and launched his political identity.
