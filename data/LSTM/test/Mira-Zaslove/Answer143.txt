24K = the purest gold. Bright yellow and very soft and expensive. Most Americans have never even seen 24k gold jewelry. It is very rare in the US  because it bends  dings  dents  and scratches easily.22k = ~ 92% pure gold. The standard in the high-end Indian  Asian  and Middle Eastern markets. 22k gold is very vivid and has a bright yellow color. It's more exquisite and valuable for investment. 18k = ~ 75% pure gold. The standard in the European market and the choice for high-end fine jewelry in the US.
