  Ultimately  they would both be psychological in that the deal with the mind  would they not? Certainly  our understanding of the ego  starts with Freud's work in defining the formation of an abstract idea of the self  based in reaction to our earliest experiences  fears  sublimation  projection  dissociation  and transference  and this "abstract entity " we refer to as the "psychological ego." It was great pioneering work and survives to this day as the absolute best way to describe the abstract inner working of the mind  and the normal and abnormal development of each individual's psyche or persona.
