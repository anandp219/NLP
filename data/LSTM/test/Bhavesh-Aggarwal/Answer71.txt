Jog for 800 meters  or do a few dozen jumping jacks. Swimming  biking climbing  all of the low intensity activities that elevate your heart rate are ideal for this phase. This will get your circulation up and running  warm up your muscles  and mentally put you in the zone. After the intial warming up  continue with dynamic stretching. Here's a list of stretches you can do:http://www.brianmac.co.uk/dynami...Do not static stretch before your sessions! Ideally  do static stretching around 3-4 hours after your workouts  or on the days you're not working out.Once you've warmed up with low-intensity stuff and some dynamic stretches  do 2 warm up sets with low reps of an easy exercise.
