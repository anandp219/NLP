Your shoulders  upper chest  triceps  core  back and all other muscles are ready for what lies ahead of them. Get in front of a wall  and kick up. Slowly descend  until your arms are slightly bent. Pause for a moment  then push back up. That's a half handstand push-up. Don't forget to place a towel beneath your head!Repeat the half handstand push-up  until you think you've got one or two reps more left. Stop the set there. Repeat again after about 2 minutes.After two sets of half handstand push-ups  practice handstand push-up negatives.
