By the time that Germans started to come in large numbers  in the mid-19th century  there were already millions of English speakers throughout the eastern part of North America into which they could be quickly assimilated.The situation with Hispanics was very different  almost the reverse. English speakers arrived in the Southwest very late -- the first came to the easternmost areas of Texas only in the early 19th century  about three centuries after the Spanish-speakers did.
