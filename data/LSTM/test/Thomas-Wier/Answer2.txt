Sixe..: qualite  comparison  gendre  noumbre  fygure  and case.Because gender still retains this original meaning of an abstract grammatical category  it is quite reasonable to talk about nouns in a language having multiple genders to refer to nouns that are masculine  feminine  animate  inanimate  rational  irrational  long  short  high  low  round  flat  etc. Most languages in the world do not grammaticalize such concepts as formal categories  but in those that do  it is quite common for a language to have five or more genders  as you can see in this map from WALS:The record comes from the Arapesh language of Papua New Guinea  which has at least nineteen genders in some varieties.
