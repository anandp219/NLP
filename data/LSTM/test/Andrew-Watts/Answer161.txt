The Often Mechanical Approaches to the Voice (Relinquishing Control vs. Intervening Craft) A major commonality amongst these works is the reliance (to varying degrees) on compositional processes or systems  relinquishing creative control over certain micro level aspects. I am sitting in a room has no intervention by the composer on how the material is crafted  only the concept of the work itself features decision making solely allocated to Lucier. The choice of text  though often is Lucier's in practice  need not be.
