If the prompter notices him first the SX card will be displayed to the ensemble and those who are playing fade out while those who are not playing fade in. Another musician  hearing something she likes  might place one hand on her head and hold up one finger with the other hand cueing the prompter to display the 1 card. This card initiates the sound memory 1 event where musicians are to remember what they are playing at that moment and be able to immediately reproduce and repeat it for an extended period the next time the 1 card is displayed.
