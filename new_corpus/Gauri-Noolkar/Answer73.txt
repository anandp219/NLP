Tolerance.The religion bit is not that new any more  but the Indian tolerance towards all that is unpleasant is quite a characteristic. From nosy aunts  to ill-built roads  to loud babies in theatres  to irregular milkmen and maid-servants  to corrupt bureaucrats  to blatant racism  to openly ridiculing physical deformities  to shamelessly peeping into others' personal lives - we have it and accept it all.
