  Article 16 (2) makes a general rule. Article 16 (3) carves out an exception to that. So  domicile requirement is legal and constitutional. And I will tell you the purpose. That being born in a place  having a house and belonging to a state should matter in getting educational and employment opportunities. This is beneficial looking at a broader picture.
