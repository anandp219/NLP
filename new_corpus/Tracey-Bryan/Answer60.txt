(1) Everyone has the right to own property alone as well as in association with others. (2) No one shall be arbitrarily deprived of his property.Everyone has the right to freedom of thought  conscience and religion; this right includes freedom to change his religion or belief  and freedom  either alone or in community with others and in public or private  to manifest his religion or belief in teaching  practice  worship and observance.Everyone has the right to freedom of opinion and expression; this right includes freedom to hold opinions without interference and to seek  receive and impart information and ideas through any media and regardless of frontiers.
