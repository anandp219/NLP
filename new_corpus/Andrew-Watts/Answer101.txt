According to Zorn  both Ives and Stalling display a level playing field for every genre to coexist in  no style or practice is used in a way to imply elitism or division between low art and high art.Lastly  there is the possibility that Zorn was indeed influenced by other composers working with polystylism. In the 1971 essay "Polystylistic Tendencies in Modern Music" author Alfred Schnittke  himself considered to be a leading figure in polystylism  cites the a number of composers utilizing this concept in their work(s).
