Canone alla Settima with the slow lament bass immediately following the virtuosic two-part toccata of the preceding variation. Bach never composed an opera  but with this impassioned embellishment of the Aria bass line he is perhaps commenting on the prevalence of this convention and its association with  as Purcell described  love in seventeenth-century opera.
