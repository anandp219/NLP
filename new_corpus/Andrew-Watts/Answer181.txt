Silvia Vizzardelli and Marco Maria Tosolini respectively analyse the concepts of anamorphosis and fall in Romitellian poetics and the parallelism between Romitelli  Zappa and Hendrix.Moving past the shared technical roles of the soloist in Hendrix and Romitelli  there is a different reception of the guitarist by the audience in Trash TV Trance. Apart from the typical venue being a concert hall rather than a rock stage  with Hendrix and Zappa the focus is on the artist as a quasi cult of personality to convey musical drama.
