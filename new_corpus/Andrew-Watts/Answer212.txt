Terminology: At the most basic level there are different terms used to describe the pitch clustering in Ligeti's and Penderecki's large-scale works of the 1960s. Ligeti coined the term micropolyphony when discussing the processes of his music  and the term  as well as the technique  has become inextricably linked to him.
