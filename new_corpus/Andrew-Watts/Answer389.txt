Fortunately  Rachmaninov  Respighi  and Editions Russe all agreed  and the orchestration of five of them soon came about.Earlier  Rachmaninov had kept the visual inspiration of the tudes-tableaux secret  saying  "I do not believe in the artist discussing too much of his images. Let them [the audience] paint for themselves what it most suggests." But with the Respighi orchestration project settled  he wrote his Italian colleague  "Will you permit me  Matre  to give you the secret explanations of their composer?"
