For instance  the use of excerpts from Anders' To Be or Not To Be: Diary of Hiroshima and Nagasaki was so critical to the identity of the work that not only did Nono use it for the composition's subtitle (Sul ponte di Hiroshima // On the Bridge of Hiroshima)  but actually wrote into the score the lines beyond what he set for the voices as a 'guide' to the conductor and players. The second movement  Djamila Boupacha  moves from suffering on a massive scale represented by the full ensemble to suffering on an individual scale.
