She says that the only reason she is not very good at it is because she does not practice  inferring that he must practice being social more if he wishes to become proficient at it. This statement is met with gaiety by Elizabeth's companion  Colonel Fitzwilliam  but Darcy himself appears mortified by her suggestion  and rightly so. It's sort of like telling someone who is afraid of heights to practice jumping out of planes in order to overcome their discomfort. This casts Mr. Darcy in a different light.
