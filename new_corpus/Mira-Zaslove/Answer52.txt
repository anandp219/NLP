Ask if anything about the culture turned out to be different than they were expecting. 2. What does the office typically look like at 7am and 9pm? You want to find out what times people are at the office. Often the expected company hours given during interviews are not realistic. For instance  I've worked at places that scared interviewers telling them to be prepared to work their butts off late at night  when if fact  the office was a ghost-town past 6pm. I've also worked places that said they valued "balance " during the interview  but the office was often buzzing well past 9pm on Sundays.3.
