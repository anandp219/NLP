Good companies will do this for you. However  once the GC process starts  it takes some time  and is complicated  with many steps. Yo have no control over how quickly it will get done. And switching companies while you are in the process of getting your GC can be scary  so many Indian engineers generally stay with the same company until they get the GC-- which can take up to 7-8 years. Once you fianlly have your GC  then you are a free bird and can move around. There won't really be any issues with your job prospects or types of companies ready to hire you.
