  The Parents' Club of Palo Alto & Menlo Park (PAMP) is an awesome online community for Parents in Silicon Valley. http://pampclub.org/site/ PAMP is volunteer-led and non-profit. PAMP has been around for almost 25 years and has membership of around 2 000 parents in Silicon Valley. I found our Nanny on PAMP  and have used PAMP as a good resource for activities  for both Mom's & Dad's  in Silicon Valley. PAMP is a great community resource for things like the following:Events: PAMP organizes both large and small community events.
