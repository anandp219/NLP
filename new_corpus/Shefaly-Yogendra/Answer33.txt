Why might it not be a magic bullet? Because weight management is complicated -- much more than the easy verbiage around "eat less  move more" may have us believe. The molecular regulation of appetite is mediated by several hormones all of which are correlated with weight  stress and genetics. Contrary to what we think a large person actually needs more calories to just sustain than a leaner person of the same height  age  gender may need for the same level of activity.
