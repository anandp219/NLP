  The original road signs were designed by Jock Kinneir and Margaret Calvert. Kinneir originally (and successfully) designed the easy to read white typography on a blue background motorway signs in 1958:The Worboys report  formally known as Traffic signs for all-purpose roads  was a report commissioned in 1962 that outlined changes to the British traffic signing system. Its fundamental aim was to review signs that were considered to be outdated and difficult to use in an era of faster motoring and higher quality roads.
