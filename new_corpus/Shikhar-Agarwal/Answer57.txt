  Lord Shiva perfectly knew that Ganesh was his son and Ganesh knew that Shiva was his father. A lot of people have questions about dharma: who makes it  is it the same for everyone and so on. Let's dive into mythology to find some answers.Some time after ocean-churning (Samudra manthan)  Shiva realized that his great "sacrifice" of drinking poison was in vain - people were misusing money and power  and disorder was prevailing in the society. So he went on penance to think about the situation. During his absence  the situation got worse.
