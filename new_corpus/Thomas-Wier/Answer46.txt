But after writing  as the process of copying the dispositions of signs on a tablet was introduced  Near Eastern art became linear and thus could tell a story. [When Writing Met Art]An example of this is the Kish Tablet  found in the Sumerian city of Kish and dated to ca. 3500 BC:The Kish TabletOn this tablet  there is only an evocation of a narrative (of some kind); there is no true telling of that narrative  since there is no way to relate signs to fully inflected spoken words.
