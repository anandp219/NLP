The people you meet on Quora or Facebook might appear to you to be either profoundly socially awkward or those rainbow of light people  but sometimes aspects of these impressions are cultural and sometimes this is PR image crafting and sometimes it is just site self-selection. It's easy to think there is something in the water  but maybe the size and shape of your pond are just changing. The majority of people are neither this nor that  they are somewhere in the middle. Most of the time  however  we happen to bump into people in one narrow channel of their life which skews our perspective.
