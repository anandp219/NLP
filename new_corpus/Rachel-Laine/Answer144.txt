In high school many people tend to think  in a rather insular way  that all of their friends must attend their high school or be a member of this clique or that clique. This kind of thinking isn't "branching out" thinking  it's  well  ... the opposite of that. And it's not a great way to think when you want to broaden your horizons and expand your circle. Are all of your parent's friends their coworkers?? Of course not! That seems silly  right? Branching out of the walls of your high school has several perks  including the ever-coveted college app shiny star.
