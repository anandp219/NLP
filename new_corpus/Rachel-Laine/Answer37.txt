Sleep deprivation interferes with almost all aspects of health and development  including mental health  relationships  and academic performance. Losing out on sleep alone could color a student's outlook on school. Autonomy: When was the last time you had to ask for permission to use the restroom? When you have a horrible boss do you have the choice to leave if you find a different job? Students have shockingly little autonomy  yet crave autonomy just as much as you or I do. Personal freedom and choices - even if they are false choices - make us feel better about ourselves.
