Bow PullsHold on to a vertical bar (goal post  lamp post  pipe)  with one arm at chest height and the other at face height. Bend your arms slightly so that you are around 2/3 of an arm's length away from the bar. Now push with the upper arm and pull with lower one as hard as you can for five seconds. Then  without a break  push with the lower arm and pull with the other one  again for five seconds.
