You may lower yourself fast or slow  but it must ALWAYS be under control. Depending on the exercise  you are either meant to pause at the bottom for a moment  or bounce back up directly. This action too  must be controlled. As a rule of thumb  when training for strength  you are steering every single moment of the exercise. Take 2 seconds for moving up and down  wait for 1 second at the top of the movement  and where applicable  wait for 1 second at the bottom as well.Don't forget the BasicsEspecially when it comes to calisthenics  you don't want to forget the basic three: two-legged bodyweight squats  two-armed medium grip push-ups and two-armed medium-grip pull-ups.
