Reps  Sets  RestOne thing many people ask is: How many reps and sets should I do? How much do I rest between sets?The answer is  as so often  "It depends." It depends on whether you want mostly strength and little size  size and strength  size  or endurance. Here are some rough guidelines on the matter  plus diet advice:Bhavesh Aggarwal's answer to How can a very skinny  24 year-old grow muscles?When training for pure strength  keep the breaks long  2-5 minutes. The more you're working on size or even endurance  the shorter the breaks become.
