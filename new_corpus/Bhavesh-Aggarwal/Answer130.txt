Remember that a kip-up is both a power feat as well as a skill. As such  even if you have everything else dialled in  getting the sequence right is crucial to success. You should always practice kip-ups on a soft surface  grass is ideal. When starting out  train the kip up on stretch of grass that has an incline  with your hands higher than your feet.
