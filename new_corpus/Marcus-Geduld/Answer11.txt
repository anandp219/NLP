Being too loyal might have decreased our ancestors' chances of reproduction; being too promiscuous might have blunted their mating chances  too. Each person--and each couple--finds its own way to cope with conflicting impulses. (Throttling  cheating  fantasizing  divorcing  allowing...) It's not the case that all married couples cease flirting. Nor is it the case that flirting causes disharmony in all marriages. I am not much of a flirt  but that's because I'm introverted in general. My wife is a party girl who flirts with everybody.
