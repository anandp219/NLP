  Lucy is essentially an attempt by director Luc Bresson to apply the aesthetics of a film movement named Cinma du look  of which he is a proponent  to a sub genre of horror called Body horror to produce a mainstream action-thriller whose plot is held up using Hollywood cliches about the human brain.Even if there were any truth to any of the neuroscience presented in the movie  which there is not  to look for a meaning in the film conflicts with the philosophy that guides the film  which is an elevation of style over substance.
