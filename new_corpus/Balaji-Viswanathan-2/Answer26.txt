  Gupta empire is the longest of major Indian empires extending for nearly three centuries. Mauryan empire lasted about 150 years; Mughal and British Raj each lasted slightly less than 200 years each. It had among the strongest of militaries and most efficient of administration among any Indian empire.It is in Gupta dynasty in which India had some of its greatest achievements. From chess to sex (kamasutra)  from zero to Shakuntala  from Panchatantra to ayurvedha  the key elements that defined Indian history were made then.
