  There are two episodes in the Bangladeshi Liberation War. The one between March 1971 [when Mujibur was arrested] and end of November 1971 was the civil war. India while supporting Mukti Bahini and other rebel movements  was not officially a part of it.The two weeks between December 3 and 16 of that year was the Indo-Pakistan war. Indo-Pakistani War of 1971. Pakistan started it by sending a bunch of jets to attack India and India hit back. Both countries involved their navy  airforce and army. India captured territory and armed forces.
