It is supposed to be informal  which is why it does not include kata in its name. Uke attacks with hits  kicks  knife and stick attacks as well as with a gun. Tori responds with appropriate self-defence techniques.J no kata performs soft techniques. Many techniques include uke's attacks followed by a counter move  another attack ending finally to the preparation phase of the throw. Uke is not thrown but he is taken into control so that the throw could be conducted if wanted.Koshiki no kata is based on ancient armour techniques.
