The primary examples are food  shelter  clothing. However at the same time  to provide services to these citizens someone has to produce these services. So to provide food to the people  someone has to cultivate grains; to provide housing  someone has to build the houses. Thus we've another concept at loggerheads: cost of producing these services. As a society  the aim should be to ensure everyone has a basic minimum of services while compensating everyone for the services they provide accordingly.
