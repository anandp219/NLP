One such field that really requires your skills is that of development. Yes  the subject of Development Economics rings a bell here  doesn't it? With your degree in Economics  you could work on a number of issues which directly affect millions of people on the planet. You could work with population  children  women  refugees  health and hygiene  water and sanitation  community development  environment  agriculture  education  growth and employment  sustainability - you name it.A good Economics degree would arm you with the tools and the thought process that would enable you to work comfortably and efficiently in most of these areas.
