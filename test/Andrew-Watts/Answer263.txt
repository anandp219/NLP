Lastly  a major influence inherent in the complex canons was mathematics  and though this is not technically a musical influence it did shape compositional thought the 15th century. Paul Kinsman in Platonic and Pythagorean Ratios in the Formal Analysis of 15th Century Music highlights this crucial aspect of Ockeghems Missa Prolationum: This mass typifies a musical composition whose structure suggests a link with the type of celestial mathematics that pervades 15th C composition.
