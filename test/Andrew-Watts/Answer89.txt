Performers can  and have on various occasions  disregard the given text and make their own of any length and content. The piece works regardless of the text  albeit taking on a slightly different individual character for each interpretation. What transformed Ursonate into a musical performance was this human interpretation  however  for I am sitting in a room it is the opposite and the most musical qualities are attributed to the non-human electronic transformations. These transformations of speech into more perceivable music parameters are made possible by the physical properties of each acoustical space.
