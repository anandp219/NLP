A few that come to mind are Dry Creek Olive Company (Trattore Farms)  B.R. Cohn  and the Olive Press.Tour Lagunitas Brewery and find out why one of their brews was censored or have a pint of Pliny the Younger at Russian River Brewery. Spend a day on the water: Go kayaking or canoeing on the Russian River. Eat at one of Napa's great restaurants. French Laundry is an institution  but Napa is practically bursting at the seams with great places to eat.Enjoy a spa day. Many of the hotels in wine country have great spas.
