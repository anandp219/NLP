I think that is a good question and something to keep in mind. Obviously  dont be a know it all or start lecturing your prospective co-workers. However  if you are knowledgeable about something  and can show passion and interest in a topic  do it. Be prepared to talk about yourself and your interests. Have answers ready to common questions like: what books or online publications you read  what TV shows or movies you watch  your hobbies  what you like to do on the weekends  etc.
