  "Give Me Liberty or Give Me Death." Patrick Henry at the Virginia Convention in 1775(Picture Source: Patrick Henry Center For Individual Liberty)Patrick Henry (1736  1799) served as the first and sixth Governor of Virginia and was a Founding Father of the United States. Henry was a rousing orator and popular political figure during the Revolutionary Period. His memorable one-liner is one of the defining credos of the American Revolutionary War. Henry's words were first proclaimed at the Virginia convention  and helped push the delegates  including Thomas Jefferson and George Washington  to battle.
