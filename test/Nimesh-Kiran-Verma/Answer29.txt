Get thee down  for this is for thy good  O sinless one! "Pandu's brave son Dhananjaya did as he was directed. The intelligent Krishna  abandoning the reins of the steeds  then dismounted from the car of Dhananjaya. After the high-souled Lord of all creatures had dismounted from that car  the celestial Ape that topped the mantle of Arjuna's vehicle  disappeared there and then. The top of the vehicle  which had before been burnt by Drona and Karna with their celestial weapons  quickly blazed forth to ashes  O king  without any visible fire having been in sight.
