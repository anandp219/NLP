They are not born with a non-creative gene. So why are Indian students generally non-creative? Let's get back to the bird example.Parent birds endeavor to make their children independent. If a child is kept in protection till the age of 22  he has already lost his best years of creativity. In the West  a child from the beginning is taught to be independent.
