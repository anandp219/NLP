The 'prompter'  though not making any sound  could effectively be improvising. The players have not rehearsed the transitions and exact timings  plus without written parts there is bound to be differences in performance even among those with solidified musical responses to each symbol. Aspects such as the exact number of musicians  instrumentation  and duration of the piece as a whole are left as indeterminate.
