This crossover appeal won Cash the rare honor of multiple induction in the Country Music  Rock and Roll  and Gospel Music Halls of Fame.Mozart also has a great deal of crossover appeal  and wrote in every major genre existing during his lifetime  including symphony  opera  the solo concerto  chamber music including string quartet and string quintet  and the piano sonata. In the end  it is important to remember that no creative figure exists as an island. The influence of Mozart is massive and you will be able to find any isolated characteristic inherent to his music in the works of his successors (and sometimes his predecessors).
