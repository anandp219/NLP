So  in this case  there is some merit in "fake it till you make it " as we tend to grow through experience  and the lengthy psychoanalysis is not always necessary nor as efficient as letting the mind heal itself through living. Accepting higher values of who we are inevitably moves us toward who we are and strengthens those values. Meditation  being vigilant of our thoughts  having a willingness to grow  all help condition the mind toward being aware of motivation  toward being capable of choosing better the next time.
