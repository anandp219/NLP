"I'm less impressed with someone who closes a $2 million deal alone than I am with someone who brought all of us in and still closed the same $2 million deal " says David. When I was first promoted to sales manager  inexperience  and my salesman's ego made it difficult for me to share  and ultimately  give away the spotlight. I was resistant to pass on my best clients.
