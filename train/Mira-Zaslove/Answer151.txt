Fraud and the fees associated with accepting payment can easily wipe out many sellers. The marketplace will need to have this figured out to get sellers to feel comfortable using it. Companies like  Square and PayPal exist solely to help sellers get paid. Payment is such an enormous market  with lots of pain points  that the biggest companies in the valley are taking it on: Apple Pay - Apple  Google Wallet  and Facebook Payments.Buyers also need to feel secure with the system before they will start using it  so the service needs to make buyers comfortable.
