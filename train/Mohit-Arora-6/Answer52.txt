Everyone knows that in order to improve your game you need to play with people who are better players than you. In fact  a lot of good Indian table tennis players  including A. Amal Raj  have spent their early days in Ajmer academy. You get to play in one of the finest table tennis stadiums in India. Ajmer table tennis stadium is HUGE  with 12 table tennis tables and a seating capacity of more than a 100 people. You get free boarding  lodging and schooling. They will even bear the expenses of your tuition and other miscellaneous activities.
