As such  product and marketing functions serve the same common goal. Many answers here refer to software and hardware. In a consumer goods context  the "creative decision" resides with the creative director. This person has a set of skills and experience  that encompass an understanding of the historicity of trends  knowledge of key cultural influences at the time of making the decision  deep familiarity with consumer preferences and psychology and use contexts  deep understanding of the brand signature and values  a robust knowledge of materials  and sometimes  a person or a few persons in mind who can be persuaded to become unofficial product champions.
