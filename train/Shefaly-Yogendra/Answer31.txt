  Here is the fun thing about six packs: everyone has them. Most however are so protective of their six packs that they coddle it in a layer of fat. So essentially your question boils down to: what is the quickest way to get a six pack without having to follow a diet? And is there even a way to achieve that? The answer to the second part is short: no. For fat loss  you are best advised to take a nutritionist's advice and follow a diet regimen that keeps you energetic enough to cope with your life and encourages your body to lose its stored fat  till the six pack reveals itself in its glory.
