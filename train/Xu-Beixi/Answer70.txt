We move people by their psychological levers  not their physical ones.If one was to quiet a Disruptive student  the thing to do is the exact opposite. To give them as little notice as possible (especially if you suspect they are disruptive precisely for the attention)  use positive rather than negative sanctions  and level with them if all that did not work. It would also be for the benefit for the rest of the class  which suffer the consequences of this squabble.As the Korean proverb goes  if you kick a stone in anger  you'll hurt your own foot.
