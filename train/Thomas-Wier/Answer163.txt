(Page on und.edu)Such cases are controversial  because they call into question some of the most fundamental tenets of 20th century linguistic theorizing about grammatical universals. It will probably be a long time before we have enough data from enough languages to make a definitive statement about whether nouns and verbs are truly universal categories in all languages -- or not.
