Languages not only had absolute rarity indices  but relative ones as well in order to identify geographic clusters of languages with relatively large numbers of unusual features  resulting in this map:In this map  the darker the dot  the more that language is surrounded by languages which also have high rarity indices. What do we find? We find that most Indo-European languages from Europe have high numbers of rare features  comparable to northern Australia  the Pacific Northwest of North America and the Bolivian Amazon.
