Typical European languages act this way: just think of German  Latin or Russian. However  in some languages  additional conditions have to be met before an agreement controller will actually trigger agreement in its target. In the Georgian language  for example  only animate or motile nouns can trigger plural agreement; if the noun is not animate and cannot move on its own  then it triggers only singular agreement on the verb  while still being marked overtly as plural on the noun:Animate nouns: (1) k'ac-eb-i dga-nan (*dga-s) ezo-i man-PL-NOM stand-3PL (*stand-3sg) courtyard-in 'The men are standing in the courtyard.
