He was required to sit on a gunny sack which he had to take home with him.If I were in his position  I would have given up. I guess that was the whole point. The upper castes systematically excluded the Dalits from education  making it difficult to downright impossible at every level for them to achieve education and social status. To this day we have teachers forcing Dalit children to clean toilets in India. And a century ago  this man was able to fight it out and reach the top.After that competition  I went on with my life and did not think of Ambedkar more than the occasional passages about him in my textbooks - Until I was in college and decided to watch the biopic Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar (love you and miss you  DC++).
