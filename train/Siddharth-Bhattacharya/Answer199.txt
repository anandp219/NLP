Education is considered high returns on investment for society. Not just in the form of visible returns like productive labor  but also invisibly as a moral  responsible member of the society. Due to the perceived essential nature of the commodity  governments around the world have debated whether privatizing it is a good method or rather engaging taxpayer's money. A major problem with privatizing education (irrespective of student's background) is with inflated costs (without subsidies) a larger percentage of population would opt to invest resources in lower-skill tasks- fields which landing a job quickly.
