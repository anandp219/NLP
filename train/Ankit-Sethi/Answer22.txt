In a stunt unthinkable today  he pitched his "dream" to studio executives and produced 3 Women  a fable western where the women play russian roulette with feminine archetypes. The story is of Pinky  Millie and Willie - Pinky is a young woman of inane conversations of vapid interests  who finds a job at a health spa out west and ends up enamored of Millie  who is as grown up as she is not taken seriously at all. She considers herself a modern woman  with an apartment and a variety of consumer goods purchased by her hard-won salary  but who is ignored by men  as her flirtatious forays often ring false and her personality comes off as superficial.
