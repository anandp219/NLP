Technological determinists believe that "'You can't stop progress'  implying that we are unable to control technology" (Lelia Green). This suggests that we are somewhat powerless and society allows technology to drive social changes because  "societies fail to be aware of the alternatives to the values embedded in it [technology]" (Merritt Roe Smith)."
