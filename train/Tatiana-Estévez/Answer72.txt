The less employees you have  the better the ratio needs to be for desks to employees. However if you have a large number of employees it is less risky to assume that not everyone will be in every day and still everyone will get a desk.Booking system. The typical system is to have an electronic system where you book your desk in advance. This guarantees that you will get a desk when you go to work  or you know not to go in. What tends to happen is that people book the same desk for weeks in advance  regardless of whether they are 100% sure they need it.
