It was a big switch. I wouldn't throw the baby out with the bathwater though. Just because priorities shift  doesn't mean that she no longer values your friendship. It is important to be honest with yourself about your expectations for your bridesmaids. Do you expect them to be really involved with the day-to-day wedding prep and planning? To come to all of your dress fittings and help with putting together all of the wedding favors? To shop for matching shoes and hair accessories and nail polish?
