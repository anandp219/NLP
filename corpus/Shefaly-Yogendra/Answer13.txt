 Short of cosmetic surgery, specifically liposuction and constructive surgery for the loose skin, there is no way to lose 6 inches in a week from anywhere on the body. There is also no way to do "spot reduction" of fat or trim extra inches from one's body. For healthy and sustainable weight loss, there are several questions with great answers on Quora. Any useful advice should take into account your height, weight, age, gender, ethnicity as well as your starting weight and your general health status.
