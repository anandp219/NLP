 You can pore over specs of individual games, but there's an easier way that's pretty reliable: figure out (based on specs, most likely) in which year your machine would have been considered a mid-to-high-end gaming rig, and you can safely buy games from that year or earlier. Gog.com has lots of older games that have been made to work on modern operating systems.Of course, that'll mean you miss out on modern games that would work on your hardware; for those you'll need to look at their system requirements.
